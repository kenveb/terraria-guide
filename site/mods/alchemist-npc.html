AlchemistNPC

Stop farming potion ingredients. Just buy them.

AuthorVVV101
TypeQuality of Life
WorkshopSteam →

The Problem It Solves

In vanilla Terraria, potions are powerful but tedious to get. Each boss attempt consumes 5-10 buff potions. Restocking them means farming herbs (with specific bloom conditions), fishing for specific fish, hunting for specific enemy drops, and crafting everything at an alchemy station. It can take 30+ minutes just to re-buff for another boss attempt.

AlchemistNPC solves this by adding NPCs who sell every potion, material, and boss summon item — using money you already earn naturally.

Key NPCs

  • Alchemist — sells every potion and herb. Arrives early
  • Brewer — sells potion ingredients (fish, flowers, rare components)
  • Jeweler — sells gems and gem-related accessories
  • Young Brewer — sells boss summon items (no more farming voodoo dolls and lenses)
  • Architect — sells building blocks and furniture in bulk
  • Operator — sells wiring and mechanism items

Best Paired With

AlchemistNPC + Magic Storage = zero inventory friction. Buy what you need, dump into storage, craft everything from one interface. This combo eliminates 80% of Terraria's grinding.

Who Should Use It

  • Players with limited playtime who don't want to spend half their session farming herbs
  • Anyone attempting boss rematches (buy summons instead of crafting them)
  • Veterans replaying the game who want to skip the grind
