Calamity Mod

The largest and most ambitious Terraria expansion ever made

AuthorFabsol & Team
TypeContent + Overhaul
WorkshopSteam →

Why It's #1

Calamity Mod is the most downloaded Terraria mod for a reason. It adds more content than the base game's Hardmode expansion — 25+ unique bosses, 5 new biomes, 2000+ items, and a post-Moon Lord endgame that can take another 30+ hours. If you've finished vanilla Terraria and want more, Calamity is where you start.

What It Adds

  • 25+ Bosses — from the Desert Scourge to Supreme Calamitas
  • 5 New Biomes — Sulphurous Sea, Abyss, Brimstone Crag, Sunken Sea, Astral Infection
  • Rogue Class — new throwing-based class with stealth mechanic
  • Difficulty Modes — Revengeance Mode and Death Mode for extreme challenge players
  • Post-Moon Lord Boss Rush — Providence, Devourer of Gods, Yharon, and more
  • New Ore Tiers — Aerialite, Cryonic, Perennial, Scoria

Difficulty Warning

Calamity is much harder than vanilla. Even on Normal difficulty, expect to die often. The mod is balanced around its own boss patterns and expects mastery of dodge mechanics. New players should start on Normal with a melee build.

Who Should Play It

  • Players who have completed vanilla Terraria at least once
  • Those who enjoy challenging boss fights with complex patterns
  • Anyone who wants a "Terraria 2" amount of new content

Similar Mods

If Calamity is too hard, try Thorium for a more balanced vanilla-like expansion. For extreme challenge, combine with Fargo's Souls.
