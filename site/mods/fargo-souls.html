Fargo's Souls Mod

The ultimate challenge for Terraria veterans

AuthorFargowilta & Team
TypeContent + Difficulty
WorkshopSteam →

What Makes It Special

Fargo's Souls is two mods in one: the Enchantment System (collect all accessories from a class and craft a super-accessory with all their effects) and Eternity Mode (every vanilla boss gets new attack patterns, increased speed, and devastating new phases — even King Slime becomes terrifying). There's also the Masochist Mode for when Eternity isn't hard enough.

Key Features

  • Enchantments — combine full accessory sets into one slot. 30+ enchantments to collect
  • Eternity Mode — remixes every vanilla boss with new AI patterns and attacks
  • Soul Accessories — combine all enchantments into ultimate Soul items
  • Mutant NPC — sells boss summons, provides boss rush quests
  • Boss Summon Shop — no more crafting summon items individually
  • Deviantt NPC — sells rare enemy drops after certain milestones

Not For Beginners

Eternity Mode expects you to know every boss pattern perfectly. Regular enemies get new abilities too. If you haven't beaten Expert Mode in vanilla, do that first. Combining this with Calamity creates the hardest Terraria experience possible.

Who Should Play It

  • Terraria veterans who find Expert Mode boring
  • Completionists who enjoy grinding for ultimate items (enchantment hunting)
  • Players who want harder boss fights without adding 2000+ new items
