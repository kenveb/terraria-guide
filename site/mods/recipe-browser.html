Recipe Browser

All crafting recipes, in-game. No alt-tabbing needed.

Authorjopojelly
TypeQuality of Life
WorkshopSteam →

What It Does

Press a hotkey (default: `H`) to open a searchable item browser. Type an item name to see its crafting recipe and all recipes that use it as an ingredient. Shows the crafting station required, exact materials, and links to related items. It also works for drops — search a boss name to see their full loot table with drop rates.

Why It's Better Than The Wiki

The Guide NPC in vanilla shows you recipes if you give him an item — one item at a time. The wiki has everything but requires alt-tabbing out of the game. Recipe Browser is instant: press a hotkey, type the item name, and see the full tree in-game. It's the single biggest time-saver for learning crafting chains in modded Terraria (where you have no idea what 2000+ new items do).

Essential For Modded

This mod is borderline required for content mods. Calamity and Thorium add thousands of items with complex crafting trees. Without Recipe Browser, you'll spend more time on the wiki than in the game. Install it before any content mod.

Who Should Use It

  • All Terraria players — vanilla or modded
  • Absolutely mandatory if you're playing with content mods
  • Pairs perfectly with Boss Checklist for the complete in-game reference experience
