Thorium Mod

The most polished vanilla-style expansion for Terraria

AuthorDivermanSam & Team
TypeContent Expansion
WorkshopSteam →

Why It's #2

If Calamity feels like "Terraria on steroids," Thorium feels like "Terraria if Re-Logic kept making updates." Every boss, item, and biome in Thorium looks and plays like it could be official content. It has the best class design of any mod — the Bard and Healer are genuinely unique playstyles, not just damage-dealers with a different color.

What It Adds

  • Bard Class — uses instruments as weapons. Inspires nearby allies with buff auras
  • Healer Class — dedicated support. Heals allies, cures debuffs, provides shields
  • 10+ Bosses — The Grand Thunder Bird, Queen Jellyfish, Viscount, Borean Strider, and more
  • New Biomes — Aquatic Depths, and biome extensions
  • Thrower Reborn — turns the removed throwing class into a full progression line
  • 80+ New Enemies and hundreds of new items

Best Mod For Co-op

Thorium is the best Terraria mod for multiplayer. The Healer and Bard classes were designed for co-op play. A team of Bard + Healer + any two damage classes is incredibly fun.

Who Should Play It

  • New modded Terraria players (it's the best introduction to modded)
  • Co-op groups looking for a support/healer dynamic
  • Players who find Calamity too difficult or chaotic
