Best Weapons in Terraria —By Stage

This guide covers the best weapons for each phase of the game, organized by class. "Best" means highest practical DPS for that stage —some endgame weapons are listed for reference even though they're far away.

Notable Mentions (Pre-Hardmode)

  • Star Cannon —technically pre-HM, uses stars as ammo. Highest raw damage of anything before the Wall of Flesh. Great for boss killing but expensive.
  • Enchanted Boomerang —available from day 1 with gold/platinum + gems. Your best early ranged attack until you get a real bow or gun.
  • Bee Gun —homing bees from Queen Bee. Very effective against the Wall of Flesh.
  • Molten Fury —best pre-HM bow for the Wall of Flesh fight.
  • Water Bolt —found in Dungeon, very strong through all of pre-HM.

Daedalus Stormbow is the Mecha Boss Melter

Daedalus Stormbow + Holy Arrows = the most famous weapon combo in Terraria. Holy Arrows rain stars from the sky, and large bosses like The Destroyer take every star. You can kill all three mechanical bosses with this setup.

Note on Endgame

Moon Lord drops each class's ultimate weapon. But Stardust Dragon and Lunar Portal Staff can be crafted before fighting Moon Lord —so summoners can enter the final fight with their best gear already in hand.
