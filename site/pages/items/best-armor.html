Best Armor Sets in Terraria —By Stage

Armor is your most important defensive investment. Each set also has set bonuses that can dramatically change your playstyle. Here's what to aim for at each stage.

Pre-Hardmode Priority

Your early game goal is Gold/Platinum → then rush to either Meteor (mage) or Molten (melee/ranged). Molten Armor has the highest defense of any pre-HM set and can carry you to the Wall of Flesh.

Lunar Fragment Armor

All four endgame sets are crafted from 36 Luminite Bars + 45 of the respective Lunar Fragment. You get these from the Moon Lord fight and the four Celestial Pillars that precede him.
