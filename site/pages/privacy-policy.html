Introduction

Terraria Guide ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at terraria-guide.uk.

Information We Collect

When you visit our site, certain information is automatically collected by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) and analytics tools:

We do not have user accounts, comment sections, or contact forms that collect personal data. If you contact us via email, we will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry.

Our site uses cookies to improve your experience and to serve personalized advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our site and other websites.
