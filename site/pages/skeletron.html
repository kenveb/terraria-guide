Skeletron Guide

PropertyDetails
HP4,400 (Head) + 2x 600 (Hands) — Normal
8,800 (Head) + 2x 1,560 (Hands) — Expert
Head Damage32 (Normal) / 64 (Expert, spinning) / 96 (Master)
Defense10 (Head) / 14 (Hands) — 25 (Head after hands destroyed)
SpawnTalk to the Old Man at the Dungeon entrance at night and select "Curse"

Must-Know: Time Limit

Skeletron must be defeated before dawn (4:30 AM). If daytime comes, Skeletron insta-kills you and flies away. The fight resets and you'll need to try again the next night.

Preparation

  • Armor: Molten armor if possible; otherwise Crimson (regeneration set bonus) or Shadow
  • Weapons: Enchanted Boomerang, Minishark with Meteor Shot, or a space gun with Meteor armor
  • Accessories: Hermes Boots, Band of Regeneration, Shield of Cthulhu (if you killed Eye on Expert)
  • Arena: Build a platform arena directly above the Dungeon entrance — 3 rows of platforms, each 80+ blocks wide
  • Potions: Ironskin, Regeneration, Swiftness, and food buffs

Strategy

Phase 1 — Destroy the Hands First

Always kill both hands before attacking the head. The hands block your attacks and deal steady damage. Each hand has 600 HP:

  • Kite both hands to one side of your arena
  • Focus damage on one hand at a time with ranged weapons
  • Dodge by dropping through platforms and circling around
  • Once a hand is destroyed, it's gone for the fight

Phase 2 — Head Fight

After both hands are dead, Skeletron's head alternates between two attack patterns:

  • Chase mode — floats toward you slowly, shoots inaccurate skull projectiles. Easy to dodge.
  • Spinning mode — head spins rapidly and charges. This is dangerous. Jump over the charge and keep moving.

The head takes more damage overall (despite higher defense once hands are gone), so this phase goes faster than it feels.

Expert Mode

In Expert, the head shoots homing skulls and spins faster. Use the Shield of Cthulhu dash to dodge spin attacks. A slimy saddle (King Slime mount) lets you bounce off enemies for extra mobility.

Rewards

  • Dungeon access — the real prize. The Dungeon contains golden chests with unique weapons (Water Bolt, Handgun, Muramasa, Cobalt Shield), furniture, and the mechanic NPC bound inside.
  • Book of Skulls (16.7%) — magic weapon that fires skulls
  • Bone Glove (Expert) — throws cross-jumping bones while using any weapon
  • Skeletron Mask / Trophy (Master)
